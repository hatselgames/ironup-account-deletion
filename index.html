Account Deletion Request – IronUp: Gym & Fitness

This page explains how to request deletion of your IronUp account and associated data.

How to request deletion

Please email us from the email address linked to your IronUp account and include:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Your username / email used in the app
  • Optional: reason for deletion

Email: YOUR_SUPPORT_EMAIL

What will be deleted

  • Your account profile (e.g., username, email if provided)
  • Your posts and uploaded images
  • Other data associated with your account in our systems

Retention

We process deletion requests within 30 days, unless a longer retention is required by law.
